More Ways to Say Hello Around the World

China: In China, people bow or nod their heads to each other.

India: In India, people put their hands together. They nod their heads and they say namaste.

Thailand: In Thailand, you put your hands together, with your arms down. Then you bow your head. This is called wai.

U.S.: In the U.S., most people shake hands. Each person puts out his or her right hand. They hold hands and sometimes move the hands up and down.
